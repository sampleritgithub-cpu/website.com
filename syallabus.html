Diploma in Engineering Syllabus and Subjects Overview Diploma in Engineering involves skills-oriented training, focusing primarily on technical expertise and engineering skills. It is a technical degree taken up at the undergraduate level. Candidates who wish to pursue the course can take up the course after completing class 10. Candidates to get admission to the Diploma in Engineering course should score at least 50% marks in the last qualifying exam from a recognized institute with physics, chemistry, and mathematics subjects as compulsory subjects. The DE syllabus consists of all the DE core subjects for various specializations, DE subjects for labs, DE subjects that are common for the specializations, and elective subjects.
